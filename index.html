2017 Playoff Pool





Welcome to the 2017 Arndt playoff pool.

The rules for this year will be the same as last year. Everyone playing will submit their picks before any games of the playoff round begin.

Players can submit their picks with the form on the 'submit picks' page. If you want to change your picks before any games have started, just resubmit your picks with the form. I will use your latest entry in the database when putting together the spreadsheet.

The scoring system will be the same as last year. You will get 3 points for calling the correct number of games. You will also get 10 points for calling the correct team for the first and second round, and 12 points for calling the correct team in the third and fourth round.
